About Me

I am a programmer and audio visual performance art enthusiast. I like to mix visuals to live music & explore techniques in digital art design.

I am passionionate about music, film & technology. This site is here for me to share that passion and connect with fellow artists.

Have a look at some of my concept work and leave me a message. I'd love to connect and create visuals for your next project.

Concepts

As I learn new techniques I like to apply them to music I enjoy in music video conepts. Narrative and abstract videos intermixed in no particular order.
I own none of the rights to any of this content, it's here to demonstrate the styles and techniques I apply as I learn. Feel free to download and use these videos as you see fit.

Visual Music

Below is a collection of audio-reactive and generative content captured live in studio practice.
Audio is analyzed & mapped to generative software to create a visualization of an audio waveform, these approaches lend themselves to live performance.

I don't mean to - Resolume Audio Reactive

Song created by me in Ableton Live using 'Klossless - I don't mean to' sample pack for the MIDI Fighter 3D. Visuals composited in Resolume and programmed with audio-reactive effects

TouchDesigner - Face Trace

Touch designer network that traces faces through a webcam input. Audio reactive effects are layered in Resolume.
